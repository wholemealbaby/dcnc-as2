## Task A Specifications
(19 marks in total) Car Sales Melbourne City has recently relocated from Richmond. The company consists of four main departments: Marketing, Administration, IT, and Sales. Currently, each of Marketing, Administration and Sales departments has 40 staff, while the fast-growing IT department has 60 staff. Assume that the company has been assigned the IP address 192.100.30.0. As a networking engineer at Car Sales Melbourne City, your task is to complete the following assignments.

Breaking down the network into 2 main subnets one which includes Marketing, Administration, and Sales and the other with IT. IT gets its own network because it will contain the most users and is "fast growing"

### 2. (2 marks) Compare those two designs by the advantages and disadvantages and explain which one is better in your opinion.
| Design # | Advantages | Disadvantages |
|---------|---------|---------|
| Subnet Design 1 | Allows growth (of about 22 users) for Marketing, Admin & Sales, Evenly spreads out the allocation per department | 'Fast Growing' IT department does not have much scalability only having 2 free slots |
| Subnet Design 2 | Gives scalability for the fast growing IT Department, allowing an extra 62 users to be added' | Not much scalability for the other 3 departments. Together they have a total of 120 users so only 6 extra slots available |

In my Opinion, Subnet design 2 is more suitable assuming that the IT department will drastically grow and that the Administration, Sales and Marketing Departments see little to no growth. This is because subnet design 2 allocates up 126 slots for just the IT department (who currently have 60 users) leaving 66 extra slots. whereas subnet design 1 only allows 62 users per subnet which would only allow 2 extra users for IT. Furthermore, and reinstating the assumption that the other 3 departments see little growth, the disadvantage of design 2 where the total 120 users from those 3 departments would only allow 6 extra users to be added, would be rendered moot.
